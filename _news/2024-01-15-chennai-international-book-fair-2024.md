Amalion was at the Chennai International Book Fair (CIBF) held between 15 and 18 January 2024 and organised by the Directorate of Public Librairies and Tamil Nadu Textbook and Educational Services, India. As a CIBF fellow, Amalion participated in the international Rights Hub and exhibited at the fair with other publishers from India and various parts of the world.
