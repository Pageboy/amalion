Amalion was invited to join a collective of independent publishers from around the world at the 5th edition of LITERAL PRO, Barcelona Radical Book Fair held between 21 and 24 May 2026 in Barcelona, Spain. 

LITERALPRO aims to provide a tailored space for publishing professionals from around the world so that they can collaborate and share information about their projects. 

The gathering was followed by the LITERAL Fair, the largest publishing fair for critical thinking in southern Europe currently in its 12th edition. It has gathered more than 15,000 people and over 100 publishers from Catalonia and abroad. More than70 writers and intellectuals from different countries presented their works in a unique setting in the city of Barcelona. Authors this year included Ilan Pappé, Mariam Barghouti, Ash Sarkar, Álvaro García Linera, Dean Spade, Mark Bray, Karim Kattan, César González, Roque Raquel Salas... among many others.
